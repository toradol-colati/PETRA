Become a Partner

Petra offers local administrations and cultural institutions a digital infrastructure to mobilize tourist micro-donations towards the restoration of architectural heritage in their territory.

How it works for you

01
Partnership
We sign a collaboration agreement. Petra provides the infrastructure, the institution identifies the construction sites.
02
Operations
QR codes installed at construction sites. Donations go directly to your account via Stripe Connect.
03
Data
You receive quarterly reports and tourist willingness-to-pay data, usable for your policy decisions.
