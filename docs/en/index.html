How It Works

Petra creates a platform for micro-mecenatism by directly connecting heritage buildings to visitors.

The Certificate

Every donation is certified and verifiable on a public distributed ledger.

◈
Unique
each certificate is derived from a hash proof associated with the donation.
▣
Verifiable
anyone can verify legitimacy by comparing the cryptographic proof against the public ledger.
§
Permanent
once anchored on the ledger, the hash proofs cannot be altered or deleted.
